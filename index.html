Program rules
1
Membership benefit excluded
Membership discounts do not stack with pocket promo offers. Always process as a discount campaign comp — never as a membership redemption.
2
Opt-in participation
Participation is optional. Practices may opt into one, two, or all three promos independently.
3
Provider discretion applies
These are clinical tools, not blanket discounts. Use judgment — offer when appropriate and in the patient's best interest.
How to process
1
Confirm eligibility
Verify the patient meets the offer threshold (B2G1 or B4G1) before the service is performed.
2
Apply the discount campaign
In Zenoti, apply the designated pocket promo discount campaign to comp the free unit. Do not use membership discount.
3
Sculptra — apply the combo
Patient buys 4 Sculptra vials, gets 1 Restylane syringe free. Comp the Restylane via discount campaign and note it in the patient record.
4
Questions? Escalate
Billing or eligibility questions go to your regional ops or practice success contact. Do not process outside the approved discount campaign method.
